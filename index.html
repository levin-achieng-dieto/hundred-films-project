WELCOME

The 100 Films Project is a groundbreaking initiative that aims to revolutionize the film industry while empowering aspiring filmmakers. It has emerged from a collaborative partnership between the esteemed African Fiction Academy, Fiction Entertainment, and 416 Productions. The project's primary objective is to produce 100 remarkable films that showcase the power of film to inspire and connect people from all walks of life. At its core, the 100 Films Project is driven by a mission to address the limitations faced by talented individuals who lack access to traditional film school programs or adequate resources. By providing unparalleled opportunities and intensive hands-on training in actual film production, the project aims to empower aspiring filmmakers and unlock their potential. The project follows a well-structured, participatory, and intensive training program. Aspiring and emerging filmmakers are immersed in active film productions from start to finish, learning critical aspects of filmmaking and acquiring the requisite skills for film production. The vision of the 100 Films Project is to provide interns and aspiring filmmakers with a highly experiential education in visual storytelling, creative problem-solving, and professional collaboration. While rooted in the film school tradition, the project embraces entrepreneurship and innovation, preparing participants for dynamic careers in the evolving landscape of video arts and entertainment.
